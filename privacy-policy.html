डेटा संग्रहण और सुरक्षा

Data Storage & Security

आपका डेटा Supabase पर सुरक्षित रूप से संग्रहीत किया जाता है, जिसमें Row Level Security (RLS) सक्षम है। इसका मतलब है कि आपका डेटा केवल आपके और आपके अधिकृत परिवार के सदस्यों द्वारा ही एक्सेस किया जा सकता है।

Your data is stored securely on Supabase with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled. This means your data can only be accessed by you and your authorized family members.

हम आपका डेटा नहीं बेचते

हम आपकी व्यक्तिगत जानकारी किसी तीसरे पक्ष को नहीं बेचते, किराए पर नहीं देते या व्यापार नहीं करते। आपका डेटा केवल आपके परिवार के भीतर Aangan ऐप की सेवाएँ प्रदान करने के लिए उपयोग किया जाता है।

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third parties. Your data is used solely to provide Aangan app services within your family.

आपके अधिकार (DPDP Act 2023)

Your Rights as a Data Principal under India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023

भारतीय डिजिटल पर्सनल डेटा प्रोटेक्शन एक्ट, 2023 के अंतर्गत आपके पास निम्नलिखित अधिकार हैं:

  • Right to access: obtain a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction & erasure: request correction of inaccurate data, completion of incomplete data, or erasure of data no longer needed for the purposes for which it was collected.
  • Right of grievance redressal: file a complaint with our Grievance Officer (below) and, if unresolved, with the Data Protection Board of India.
  • Right to nominate: nominate another individual to exercise your rights in the event of incapacity or death.
  • Right to withdraw consent: withdraw consent for any optional processing at any time. Mandatory processing (account creation, OTP delivery, family graph) cannot be withdrawn without account deletion.

अधिकार का प्रयोग करने के लिए support@aangan.app पर ईमेल करें। हम 30 दिनों के अंदर जवाब देंगे।

To exercise any right, email support@aangan.app. We will respond within 30 days.

डेटा प्रतिधारण

Data Retention

Personal data is retained for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion request to allow restore in case of accidental deletion. Audit logs (who logged in, when) are retained for 12 months for security forensics. Anonymized aggregate analytics may be retained indefinitely. Voice messages auto-expire after 30 days.

डेटा कहाँ स्टोर होता है

Where Your Data is Stored (Cross-Border Transfer Disclosure)

Aangan stores user data in Supabase managed Postgres clusters located in the AWS Asia Pacific (Mumbai / ap-south-1) region. Push notifications transit through Expo's push servers (United States) — only the device push token and notification text leave India for that hop. No other personal data is transferred outside India.

डेटा उल्लंघन की सूचना

Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a personal data breach that is likely to cause significant harm, we will notify affected users and the Data Protection Board of India in accordance with Section 8(6) of the DPDP Act, 2023, without undue delay.
