## Field checklist — what you fill before printing

Every variant needs these 9 pieces of info. Collect them once and paste in:

| Placeholder | Where to find it |
|---|---|
| `[REGISTERED OFFICE ADDRESS]` | Certificate of Incorporation |
| `[CORPORATE IDENTITY NUMBER]` | Certificate of Incorporation, 21-char CIN |
| `[COMPANY PAN]` | Company PAN card (AAAAA0000A format) |
| `[GST NUMBER]` | GST Registration Certificate, 15-char GSTIN |
| `[AUTHORIZED SIGNATORY FULL NAME]` | Board Resolution / Incorporation doc listing Directors. Given the Vi dashboard shows "Jyotsna Amit Chaurasiya" as the account holder, that's likely the authorized signatory |
| `[DESIGNATION]` | e.g., "Director", "Managing Director", "Authorized Signatory" |
| `[DIN NUMBER]` | MCA portal → company → Director details |
| `[INDIVIDUAL PAN]` | Signatory's personal PAN |
| `[OFFICIAL EMAIL]` + `[MOBILE NUMBER]` | Whatever is registered on vilpower.in PE account |

## Printing / scanning notes

- **Letterhead**: must have ReyKan IT logo, registered address, CIN, GSTIN in header or footer. If there's no letterhead template, have a one-page Word template made — takes 10 mins.
- **Signature**: wet ink blue/black pen. Digital signatures are sometimes rejected; physical scan is safest.
- **Stamp / Seal**: round company seal in blue or black ink, slightly overlapping the signature. Essential for DLT reviewers.
- **Scan settings**: 300 DPI color PDF. Convert to PDF, not JPG. File name: `LOA_Aangan_Brand_[YYYYMMDD].pdf`.
- **File size**: keep under 2 MB. If scanner produces larger, run through [smallpdf.com/compress-pdf](https://smallpdf.com/compress-pdf) before upload.

---

## After upload — what happens next

1. Vi DLT reviewers typically act on brand name submissions within **24-72 hours**.
2. Status appears on vilpower.in → **Dashboard → Brand Names** → row showing "Pending / Approved / Rejected".
3. On approval you can reference the brand in any new Content Template submission (the Brand dropdown on the template form will include "Aangan").
4. If rejected, the "Remarks" column shows the reason — most common: "name on supporting doc doesn't match brand" (fix: re-upload an additional proof like website screenshot that explicitly shows the word "Aangan"), or "letterhead not on company paper" (fix: re-scan on proper letterhead).
