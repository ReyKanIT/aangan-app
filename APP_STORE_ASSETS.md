### Description (English)
```
Aangan (आँगन) is India's first Hindi-first family social network. Replace your 10 WhatsApp family groups with one beautiful, private space designed for Indian families — so simple that even grandmothers can use it.

FEATURES:

Family Tree (वंशवृक्ष)
Build your family tree across 3 levels — parents, siblings, children, and extended family. Preserve your lineage for future generations.

Voice Messages & Voice-to-Text (आवाज़)
Send voice messages in Hindi or English. Voice-to-text converts spoken Hindi into text — perfect for elders who find typing difficult.

Panchang (पंचांग)
Daily Hindu Panchang with Tithi, Nakshatra, Yoga, Karana. Festival calendar with 50+ Hindu festivals including Diwali, Holi, Navratri.

Events & RSVP (कार्यक्रम)
Create family events — weddings, puja, birthdays. Send invitations, collect RSVPs, share event photos.

Direct Messages & Family Chat (चैट)
Private 1-on-1 DMs and group messaging within your family. No strangers, no ads — just family.

Kuldevi / Kuldevta (कुलदेवी)
Document and share your family deity traditions. Keep spiritual heritage alive across generations.

AI Family Chatbot (AI सहायक)
Ask about festivals, puja vidhi, traditions & customs. Answers in Hindi and English.

Photo Sharing (फोटो)
Share family photos with audience control — choose who sees what.

WHY AANGAN?
• Dadi Test Passed — Large buttons (52px+), big text (16px+), Hindi-first UI
• Privacy First — No ads, no strangers, no data selling
• Phone-First — Login with phone OTP, no email needed
• Made in India — Built for Indian families with Indian traditions
• Free — Core features are completely free
```
