## App Store Listing Content

### App Name
**Aangan — Family Social Network**

### Subtitle
**परिवार से जुड़ें | Connect with Family**

### Description (English)
Aangan is India's first family social network with full Hindi voice control. Share moments, manage events, and stay connected with your family — designed so even grandmothers can use it.

Features:
- Voice-to-text in Hindi and English
- Voice commands for hands-free navigation
- Family tree with 3 levels of relationships
- Hindu Panchang and festival calendar
- Events with RSVP tracking
- Direct messaging with voice notes
- Kuldevi/Kuldevta family traditions

### Description (Hindi)
