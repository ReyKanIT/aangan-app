   - **Supporting Document:** upload the LOA PDF here

5. Submit → status goes to **Pending** → typical review 24–72 hours

## If Vi rejects again — most likely reasons + fixes

| Rejection remark | Fix |
|---|---|
| "Name on doc doesn't match brand" | Re-scan with letterhead that explicitly mentions "Aangan" in header or add a cover page with website screenshot showing the word "Aangan" |
| "Not on company letterhead" | Re-print on actual ReyKan IT letterhead — a plain Word doc with "ReyKan IT" typed at top is not enough |
| "Signature not authorized" | Attach a copy of the Board Resolution authorizing the signatory (one-page standard resolution — Kumar can ask his CA) |
| "Stamp missing" | Re-scan ensuring the round company seal is clearly visible and overlaps the signature |
| "Poor scan quality" | Re-scan at 300 DPI minimum; avoid phone-camera scans — use a proper flatbed scanner or the "Adobe Scan" app with auto-enhance off |
